# Data Analyst Salary Analysis Using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes factors affecting Data Analyst salaries using the Data Science Job Salaries dataset from Kaggle. The analysis aims to understand how experience level, employment type, company location, work setting, company size, and job specialization influence compensation.

The analysis was performed using SQL to answer several business questions and generate insights related to salary trends in the data industry.

---

## Dataset

- **Dataset:** Data Science Job Salaries
- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Records:** Data related to data science professions and salaries in USD

### Variables Used

- Job Title
- Experience Level
- Employment Type
- Company Location
- Work Setting
- Company Size
- Salary in USD

---

## Tools Used

- SQL
- MySQL

## Business Questions

1. Are there any missing values in the dataset?
2. What job positions are included in the dataset?
3. Which job titles are related to Data Analyst?
4. What is the average salary of Data Analysts?
5. How does experience level affect salary?
6. How does employment type affect salary?
7. Which country offers the highest average salary?
8. Do remote jobs pay more than onsite jobs?
9. Does company size influence salary?
10. Which Data Analyst specialization has the highest average salary?

---

## Analysis Results

### 1. Data Quality Check

No missing values were found in the dataset, indicating that the data is clean and ready for analysis.

### 2. Job Positions Available

The dataset contains 44 different professions related to data and artificial intelligence.

### 3. Data Analyst Related Roles

Several Data Analyst positions were identified, including:

- Data Analyst
- BI Data Analyst
- Business Data Analyst
- Financial Data Analyst
- Marketing Data Analyst
- Product Data Analyst
- Lead Data Analyst

### 4. Average Salary of Data Analysts

The average annual salary of Data Analysts is approximately **$93,200**.

### 5. Salary by Experience Level

| Experience Level | Average Salary (USD) |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| Entry-Level (EN) | 57,112 |
| Mid-Level (MI) | 77,941 |
| Senior-Level (SE) | 112,662 |
| Executive-Level (EX) | 130,000 |

Experience level has a significant impact on salary, with Executive-Level positions receiving the highest compensation.

### 6. Salary by Employment Type

| Employment Type | Average Salary (USD) |
|----------------|---------------------|
| Contract (CT) | 100,000 |
| Full-Time (FT) | 93,850 |
| Part-Time (PT) | 10,354 |

Contract positions have the highest average salary.

### 7. Salary by Company Location

| Country | Average Salary (USD) |
|-----------|-------------------|
| United States | 112,001 |
| Canada | 79,936 |
| United Kingdom | 52,712 |

The United States offers the highest average salary among countries in the dataset.

### 8. Salary by Work Setting

| Work Setting | Average Salary (USD) |
|--------------|--------------------|
| Fully Remote | 101,065 |
| Onsite | 84,203 |
| Hybrid | 51,239 |

Fully remote positions tend to provide higher compensation.

### 9. Salary by Company Size

| Company Size | Average Salary (USD) |
|--------------|--------------------|
| Medium | 99,399 |
| Large | 96,208 |
| Small | 45,112 |

Medium-sized companies provide the highest average salary.

### 10. Salary by Job Specialization

| Job Title | Average Salary (USD) |
|------------|-------------------|
| Data Analyst | 92,893 |
| Business Data Analyst | 76,691 |
| BI Data Analyst | 74,755 |

Among positions with at least five observations, Data Analyst has the highest average salary.

---

## Key Insights

- Executive-Level Data Analysts earn the highest average salary (**$130,000**).
- The United States offers the highest average salary (**$112,001**).
- Fully remote positions provide higher compensation than onsite and hybrid jobs.
- Medium-sized companies offer the highest average salary.
- Data Analyst positions earn more than Business Data Analyst and BI Data Analyst roles.

---

## Conclusion

Experience level, company location, and work setting are the most influential factors affecting Data Analyst salaries. Executive-level positions, fully remote jobs, and companies located in the United States provide the highest compensation among the observations analyzed.
